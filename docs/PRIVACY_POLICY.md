# Privacy Policy for HeaLyri

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## 1. Introduction

HeaLyri ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (the "Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:
- Name and contact information (email, phone number)
- Health information (appointments, medical history, health vitals)
- Location data (for emergency services and facility routing)
- Authentication credentials (email, password, social login tokens)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Usage data (features used, time spent, navigation patterns)
- Location data (GPS coordinates for emergency dispatch)
- Log data (IP address, access times, error logs)

### 2.3 Health Information
- Medical appointments and scheduling
- Health vitals (heart rate, steps, sleep hours)
- Emergency service requests and outcomes
- Medication verification data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process and manage emergency service requests
- Connect you with healthcare providers and facilities
- Schedule and manage appointments
- Send notifications and updates
- Analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
- Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- Healthcare facilities and providers (for appointment scheduling and emergency response)
- Transportation services (drivers for emergency transport)
- Cloud service providers (Firebase, Google Cloud Platform)
- Analytics and monitoring services

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
- To comply with legal obligations
- To respond to legal process or government requests
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- To prevent or investigate fraud or security issues

### 4.3 Emergency Situations
- In emergency situations, we may share location and health information with emergency responders, healthcare facilities, and authorized personnel to facilitate emergency response.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication and authorization
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and monitoring
- Secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase/Google Cloud)

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

Emergency service records and health data are retained in accordance with healthcare data retention requirements.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- **Access:** Request access to your personal information
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your information
- **Portability:** Request transfer of your data
- **Objection:** Object to processing of your information
- **Withdrawal:** Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@healyri.com

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
